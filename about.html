Why We Built This

The internet is full of platforms that promise you can earn money by watching ads, completing tasks, or "mining" some coin — and then either pay tiny amounts for weeks before locking your withdrawal, or disappear entirely. We experienced this firsthand and decided to do something different.

CreateAndEarn is funded entirely by advertising revenue. When visitors read creator content and see ads, advertisers pay us. We share that revenue with the creators who made the content and with the task earners who helped grow the platform's engagement. There is no other source of money — which means there is no secret investment scheme, no Ponzi structure, and no way for us to pay you more than we earn.

The streak system exists specifically to separate genuine daily users from people gaming the system. The harder it is to maintain your streak, the more each surviving user's points are worth when the conversion milestone hits. It's the fairest model we could design.
