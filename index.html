Questions

Frequently Asked

Not here? Email support@createandearn.site — human reply within 48 hours.

Do I really never need to invest or pay anything? ▼
Correct. There are no premium plans, no deposit requirements, no unlock fees, and no withdrawal charges from our side. CreateAndEarn makes money from advertisers and shares that money with you. That is the only revenue model. If anyone asks you to pay to earn more — that is a scam, not us.
What exactly happens when I miss a streak day? ▼
Your entire accumulated earning balance is permanently wiped and resets to $0.00. There are no exceptions, no grace periods, and no appeals for missed streak days. This rule exists to keep the task pool sustainable — only genuine daily users earn and withdraw. Make sure you complete all tasks before midnight every single day.
Why are task rates so low at launch? ▼
Because at launch, ad revenue is low. Task rates are mathematically derived from 15% of actual ad revenue divided among active earners. We will never promise more than what the platform actually earns. As traffic grows, rates grow automatically. This is the honest, sustainable model — not inflated fake promises that collapse after a few weeks.
How does ad revenue get calculated for creators? ▼
Every time an ad is displayed on a page containing your content, the advertiser pays a CPM rate. We calculate your 30–40% share of that CPM and credit it to your wallet in real time. You can see the exact breakdown — which post, how many views, which ad rate, and your exact share — on your earnings page.
Why crypto only for withdrawals? ▼
Bank transfers and local payment apps have processing fees and minimum limits that make micro-payouts (like $4–$10) extremely costly or simply impossible at our current scale. Crypto lets us process any amount globally with just the network gas fee. We plan to add more options as we grow.
Can I be both a creator and a task earner? ▼
Yes. Your account has access to both the Creator Dashboard and the Task Center. You can post content and complete daily tasks simultaneously. Many users do both — task earnings are daily, while creator earnings are passive and accumulate over time.
What if a task is not credited correctly? ▼
If a task appears completed on your end but wasn't credited, open a support ticket within 7 days. Our team reviews the system log and credits valid tasks within 24 hours. If the task was flagged for a legitimate reason (duplicate, bot detected, incomplete), we explain exactly why in writing.
How do I know this isn't a scam like other sites? ▼
Fair question. The main differences: (1) We never ask you to invest or deposit anything. (2) Task rates are mathematically tied to real ad revenue — we can't promise more than we earn. (3) Monthly financial reports are published publicly. (4) Withdrawal proofs are posted publicly. (5) The streak/reset model means we only pay genuine daily users — not bots inflating numbers. No legitimate platform needs your money to make you money.
