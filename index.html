Questions

Frequently Asked

If it's not here, email us at support@creatorpad.io — we answer within 48 hours.

Do I really never need to invest or pay anything? ▼
Correct. There are no premium plans, no "unlock earnings" fees, no withdrawal charges, and no hidden costs. CreatorPad is 100% free to join and 100% free to use. We make money from advertisers and share that money with you. That is the only revenue model.
How is ad revenue calculated for my content? ▼
Every time an ad is displayed on a page with your content, the advertiser pays a CPM (cost per thousand views). We calculate your share of that CPM at 30–40% and credit it to your wallet in real time. You can see the exact calculation for every post on your earnings page.
Can I do tasks AND be a creator at the same time? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Your account has both a Creator Dashboard and a Task Dashboard. You can earn from both simultaneously. Many of our top earners do both — they post content and complete daily tasks to maximize income.
How quickly can I withdraw? What are the limits? ▼
Minimum withdrawal is $5. Withdrawals are processed within 3–5 business days. Supported methods: JazzCash, EasyPaisa, PayPal, Wise, Bank Transfer (PKR/USD), and USDT (TRC-20). There is no maximum withdrawal limit. There are no withdrawal fees charged by us — your bank or payment provider may apply their own fees.
What happens if my task is not credited? ▼
If a task appears completed on your end but wasn't credited, open a support ticket within 7 days. Our fraud team reviews the system log for your account and credits valid tasks within 24 hours. If we determine the task was flagged for a legitimate reason, we'll explain exactly why in writing.
Is CreatorPad available in Pakistan? ▼
Yes. CreatorPad fully supports Pakistan with JazzCash and EasyPaisa withdrawals. Our platform is available in all countries except those under international sanctions. PKR and USD earnings are both supported.
Why does the platform take 50–60% of ad revenue? ▼
Platform costs include: cloud server costs (~15%), payment processing fees (~8%), our engineering and support team salaries (~20%), ad network fees (~7%), fraud prevention systems (~5%), and business margins (~5%). This is detailed in our public monthly financial reports. If costs go down, creator shares go up — it's in our interest to be efficient.
