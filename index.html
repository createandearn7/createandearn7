Questions

Frequently Asked

Not here? Email us at createandearn7@gmail.com — human reply within 48 hours.

Do I really never need to invest or pay anything? ▼
Correct. There are no premium plans, no deposit requirements, no unlock fees, and no withdrawal charges from our side. CreateAndEarn makes money from advertisers and shares that money with you. That is the only revenue model. If anyone asks you to pay to earn more — that is a scam, not us.
What exactly happens when I miss a streak day? ▼
Your entire accumulated balance and all stored points are permanently wiped and reset to zero. There are no exceptions, no grace periods, and no appeals for missed streak days. This rule exists to keep the task pool sustainable — only genuine daily users earn and withdraw. Complete all tasks and claim your points before midnight every single day.
When will points be converted to USDT? ▼
Points will be converted to USDT when the platform reaches 1 million active users. The conversion rate is calculated from total accumulated platform ad revenue divided by total surviving points from users who maintained their streak. The fewer users who maintain their streak all the way to the milestone, the higher the rate per point will be. Early, consistent users are rewarded the most.
How long does a withdrawal take? ▼
Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours of your request. We send USDT on the TRC-20 (TRON) network. Once sent, the transaction typically confirms on-chain within minutes. You will receive a transaction hash as proof. Make sure to provide your correct TRC-20 USDT wallet address — funds sent to wrong addresses cannot be recovered.
How does ad revenue get calculated for creators? ▼
Every time an ad is displayed on a page containing your content, the advertiser pays a CPM rate. We calculate your 30–40% share of that CPM and credit it to your wallet in real time. You can see the exact breakdown — which post, how many views, which ad rate, and your exact share — on your earnings page.
Can I be both a creator and a task earner? ▼
Yes. Your account has access to both the Creator Dashboard and the Task Center. You can post content and complete daily tasks simultaneously. Creator earnings are paid in real time from ad revenue. Task earner points are stored and converted at the 1 million user milestone.
How do I know this isn't a scam? ▼
Fair question. Key differences: (1) We never ask you to invest or deposit anything. (2) Task rates are tied to real ad revenue — we cannot promise more than we earn. (3) Monthly financial reports are published publicly. (4) Withdrawal proofs are posted publicly. (5) The streak system means we only pay genuine daily users — not bots. (6) Creator earnings are paid immediately from real ad revenue, verifiable per post. No legitimate platform needs your money to make you money.
